Badarpurghat, Assam

G+1 Steel Truss Industrial Shed

Structural design of a G+1 industrial shed with mezzanine office floor at Badarpurghat, Assam. The structure spans 54 ft × 72 ft with an eave height of 7.62 m and ridge height of 9.42 m. Primary framing uses ISHB 250/300 columns and ISMB 350 / ISMC 200 beams, with a cold-formed Z-purlin roof system and composite deck mezzanine slab. Full seismic (Zone V) and wind load design carried out per IS 800:2007, IS 875, and IS 1893:2016. Total steel tonnage: 32.62 T.

Ongoing
Udupi, Karnataka

Temple Pushakarini — Ghat Steps

Design of a flexible stepped retaining structure for a temple pushakarini (sacred pond) in Udupi, Karnataka. Stone block masonry construction with geotextile filter fabric separating the native loose clay from the crushed stone drainage layer — eliminating hydrostatic pressure build-up behind the facing. A PVC-coated gabion mesh launching apron at the toe protects against monsoon scour. Treads at 300mm with 230mm risers, sized for ritual use. Flexible system selected over rigid concrete specifically to accommodate differential settlement in the soft clay foundation. Working drawings issued at 1:50 scale for direct contractor execution.

The Villa Project

Architectural and interior design for a premium villa development proposed for a real estate developer in Bangalore. Features contemporary design with decorative jaali screens, embedded wPVC boards, and expansive glazing. The atrium achieves a descending spatial effect — outer plinth raised 4ft above site datum with graduated lawn levels — giving the home a cave-like sense of arrival.
